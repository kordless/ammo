Meet Schuman Consulting

Your project is missing the unique experience, attention to detail, and client service of these Schumann Consultant team members. Because Schumann Consulting offers firsthand public and private sector experience in all aspects of domestic and international commercial development, carpentry, inspections, permitting, floodplain management, health, safety, and environmental regulations, and much more.

TJ Grossi

Executive Director

TJ grew up in the construction industry helping his father as a carpenter. This passion led him to become a union carpenter, start his own business, become an HSE Manager with an international construction company, and eventually work for the City of New Braunfels.

He has served as a Chief Building Official, Building Commissioner, and Floodplain Manager in the public sector, but his passion for the private sector brought him to Schumann Consulting.

TJ is originally from the Chicago area and grateful to be a transplanted Texan. He is a proud husband, loving father of four, and avid outdoorsman.
